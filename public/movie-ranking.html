Derrick Muturi Top 5 Movies OF 2023

Best Series I have watched so far

1. All American

This is a sports based drama movie depicting the life of a young black man aspiring to play in the NFL and learning to juggle football together with his personal life.

2. A la Carte

This is a black drama movie showing the life of black people who are trying to find love and at the same time go further in their respective careers but faced with betrayal, lack of trust and cheating.

3. Lucifer

This is a sci - fi series which depicts the bible and is about how the devil was banished from heaven and got sent to earth and takes up a human form.The series entales how the devil was forced to punish people and how he is seen as all evil while infact it was just a job he was given.Lucifer finds true love in a human who does not see him as the devil but as a friend .

4. Peaky Blinders

This is an action movie based after the second world war which show three brothers who come from the war broke and decide to to become gangsters and take over birmingham and cause havoc to england.The three brothers are Thomas Shelby - the leader, Arthur Shelby - the eldest and John Shelby - the youngest.

5. Big Mouth

This is animated comedy series that show the life of kids who are just starting puberty and how the are freaky out whilest discovering themselves at the sametime and are given fictional hormone monsters to be their guides.
